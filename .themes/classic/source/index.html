Hello World!

Jan 19th, 2014 | Comments

Welcome to my new blog/website!

Special thanks to Github and Octopress for their services that enable me to do this.

For those who are new to my work, I previously had (and for now, still have) a blog with Tumblr (which can be found here), but I have been looking for alternatives as Tumblr is full of junk which gets in the way of me blogging.

I will probably keep the Tumblr blog going for a little while longer, while I test out the features of Octopress/Github Pages, but it will not last forever.

Okay, let me introduce myself:

I am HeroesGrave (not my real name, just a pseudonym).

As HeroesGrave, I am a game developer.
As !HeroesGrave, I am a gamer.

My skills lie primarily in programming, but my pixel art is improving, and although I haven’t tried to yet, I have been brought up musically, so hopefully I can make some decent audio.

I guess I also design games too, but that doesn’t take much effort, and in the case of indie game development, is usually covered by programming anyway.

I support and enjoy taking part in hacker culture (Not cracker. See here).

I hate Apple more than Microsoft.
I hate Microsoft more than Apple. Despite the semi-paradoxical joke, I hate Microsoft a little bit more.
In general, I hate anything that takes people’s freedom away.

I love Linux.
I love the Free Software movement, and when in the right place, the Open Source movement too.
(for those who don’t know the difference, see this)

I am very familiar with Java (my first and primary language), have just about learned Rust, can read but not write code in C/C++, and can generally understand what a (sane) piece of code does in most common programming languages.
